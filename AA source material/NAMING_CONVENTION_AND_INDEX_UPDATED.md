#### Module 3.3: Networking
*Focus: Building Professional Relationships*

**CRITICAL:** Read this entire module before taking action

| Exercise | Name | Key Activities | Time Est. | Output |
|----------|------|----------------|-----------|--------|
| 3.3.1 | Mindset in Practice | Study five mindsets, awareness, curiosity, etc. | 30 min | Mindset understanding |
| 3.3.2 | Art of Conversation | Learn 10 conversation principles | 30 min | Conversation skills |
| 3.3.3 | Target Information | Understand networking goals | 15 min | Information checklist |
| 3.3.4 | The Process of Networking | 5-step system, company research, contact finding | 2-3 hours | Job Prospects (Tabs 2-3) |
| 3.3.5 | Preparation | Meeting prep, negotiation basics | 30 min | Preparation checklist |

**Key Tools:** Job Prospects Spreadsheet (Tabs 2-3)  
**Resources Referenced:** 
- Bill Burnett's five mindsets⁴
- Celeste Headlee's conversation principles⁷
- Mark Granovetter's weak ties theory⁸

**The 5-Step Networking Process:**
1. Decide company type (massive/large/medium/small)
2. Build company list (5 small : 3 medium : 2 large ratio)
3. Research companies thoroughly
4. Connect with recruiters/HR
5. Build relationships with people doing your target job

**Ongoing Practice:** Daily networking, relationship building

#### Module 3.4: Research
*Focus: Deep Company & Contact Investigation*

| Exercise | Name | Key Activities | Time Est. | Output |
|----------|------|----------------|-----------|--------|
| 3.4.1 | Company Research Rounds | Research 10 companies (5:3:2 ratio) | 3-4 hours | Complete Tab 2 |
| 3.4.2 | Contact Identification | Find gatekeepers & target employees | 1-2 hours | Contact lists |

**Repeat:** Complete multiple research rounds; start with 10, then continue  
**Sources:** LinkedIn, company websites, Glassdoor, Google

#### Module 3.5: Action
*Focus: Active Networking & Relationship Building*

| Exercise | Name | Key Activities | Time Est. | Output |
|----------|------|----------------|-----------|--------|
| 3.5.1 | LinkedIn Network Growth | Send connection requests | Ongoing | Growing network |
| 3.5.2 | Informational Meetings | Schedule 19-minute coffee chats | Ongoing | Industry knowledge, referrals |
| 3.5.3 | Relationship Development | Build authentic professional relationships | Ongoing | References, opportunities |
| 3.5.4 | Professional Development | Shadow, visit workplaces, meet teams | As available | Insider knowledge |

**Critical Practices:**
- Approach with curiosity, NOT job requests
- Ask for advice and insights
- Request referrals to others
- Offer help before leaving
- Send thank you notes
- Track all interactions in Networking tab

**Goal:** Multiple quality job offers to choose from

## Quick Reference: Key Tools & Frameworks

### Original Tools (Braedon Long)
- **SOARED Framework:** Situation, Obstacle, Action, Results, Evaluation, Discovery
- **Flow Tracking Worksheet:** Energy × Focus grid
- **Ranking Grid:** Pairwise comparison tool
- **Job Prospects Spreadsheet:** 3-tab system (Research, Employers, Networking)
- **Launching Pad Worksheet:** Career path planning tool
- **Reframing Failure Worksheet:** Growth opportunity log
- **Budget Template:** Complete expense tracking
- **Routine Tracking Template:** Daily practice log

### Borrowed Frameworks (With Attribution)
- **Five Mindsets:** Curiosity, Bias to Action, Reframing, Awareness, Radical Collaboration (Burnett⁴)
- **10 Conversation Principles:** (Headlee⁷)
- **Skills Categories:** Knowledges, Self-Management, Transferable (Fine¹)
- **MBTI Framework:** E/I, S/N, T/F, J/P (Myers-Briggs²)
- **Competency Levels 1-5:** (OECD⁵)
- **Identity Story Elements:** Relevance, Clarity, Distinctiveness, Consistency (Denning⁶)
- **Weak Ties Theory:** (Granovetter⁸)

### Ongoing Practices Throughout Program
- **Part 1 Module 3 → Part 3:** Daily flow tracking

- **Part 1 Module 5 → Part 3:** Daily mindfulness (5-20 min)

## Webapp Navigation Design Recommendations

### Progressive Disclosure
- **Level 1:** Parts (3 main sections)
- **Level 2:** Modules (15 total, 5 per part)
- **Level 3:** Exercises (30-40 total)
- **Level 4:** Activities (80+ individual tasks)

### User Progress Tracking
- Completion checkboxes for each activity
- Progress bars for modules and parts
- "Continue where you left off" functionality
- Estimated time remaining

### Cross-References System
- Hyperlink all internal references (e.g., "See Exercise 1.1.1" → clickable)
- "Related exercises" suggestions
- "Prerequisites" warnings where applicable
- "You'll use this in..." forward references

### Search & Filter
- Search by keyword
- Filter by: estimated time, tool required, partner needed, ongoing practice
- Tag system: #reflection #research #writing #ranking #networking
